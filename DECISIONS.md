# DECISIONS.md

Architecture decision log. Append-only. Each entry records a non-obvious choice, the alternatives considered, and the reasoning — so future sessions don't re-litigate settled questions.

## Format

```
## ADR-NNN: <title>

- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Status:** Accepted | Superseded by ADR-MMM | Deprecated
- **Context:** what prompted the decision
- **Decision:** what we chose
- **Alternatives:** what we rejected and why
- **Consequences:** what this makes easier / harder
```

Keep entries short. Link out to `PRD.md` or `docs/` rather than duplicating.
